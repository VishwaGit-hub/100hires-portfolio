Content:
90% of sales reps are killing their deals in the inbox.

Never negotiate pricing via email.

Email negotiations are where deals go to die.

These threads turn into long, confusing conversations that could be resolved much faster over a call.

If there’s one takeaway:

Stop negotiating in email threads.

Instead:

Pick up the phone.  
Handle objections in real time.  
Walk through the proposal directly.  
Deal with pushback instead of avoiding it.  

Friction is not a bad thing—it helps uncover the truth and move deals forward.

Stop hiding behind email. Start selling.

Key Insight:
Email is effective for initiating conversations, but critical deal stages (like pricing and objections) require real-time communication to close efficiently.

Content:
Personalization in cold emails has never worked at scale.

Most “personalization hacks” focus on surface-level details like location, recent posts, or generic references.

This creates emails that feel artificial and self-centered.

The problem is not lack of personalization—it’s lack of value.

If you’re reaching out for business, answer clearly:

Why should the prospect care?  
Why should they listen?  
Why should they trust you?  

Instead of trying to be clever:

• Be direct and concise  
• Explain exactly what you do  
• Provide a clear and relevant reason to engage  
• Include a simple next step  

With AI, shallow personalization will only increase, making most outreach feel like noise.

Clear, honest, and value-driven communication will stand out.

Key Insight:
Effective cold outreach prioritizes clarity and relevance over superficial personalization, especially at scale.
