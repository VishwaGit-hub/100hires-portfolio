## Post 1
Date: 2025-07-20  
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_i-havent-sent-a-cold-email-since-2019-activity-7352893079462313984-ddqc/

Content:
I haven’t sent a cold email since 2019.

No outreach.  
No pitch decks.  
No “just circling back” follow-ups.

Not because I’m above it.

Because I built a system that made it unnecessary.

And it started with one choice:

I decided to turn my content into a moat.

Here’s what that meant:

• Content that made people feel seen  
• Consistent publishing to stay top-of-mind  
• Solving problems in public to build trust  

It didn’t explode overnight.  
It wasn’t flashy.  
But it worked.

Bit by bit, my content created leverage.

Not attention. Leverage.

Now people find my work, read for weeks, and buy when they feel ready.

I don’t chase.  
I don’t convince.  

I just publish consistently and attract the right audience.

This system became the backbone of my business:

• $10.5M in revenue  
• 42,000+ customers  
• No sales calls  

If your content gets views but not revenue, you’re missing signal—not followers.

Key Insight:
Inbound content can replace outbound only when it builds sustained trust and positioning—not just visibility.

## Post 2
Date: 2025-09-27  
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_i-sent-a-cold-dm-to-someone-i-admire-yesterday-activity-7378032826765484033-aatQ/

Content:
I sent a cold DM to someone I admire yesterday.

No ask.  
No pitch.  
No agenda.

Just appreciation:

"Your content changed how I think about entrepreneurship. Thanks."

They replied within an hour.

Most people overcomplicate networking:

• Trying to craft the perfect message  
• Waiting for the right moment  
• Trying to prove credibility first  

But high-level people:

• Are flooded with pitches  
• Are tired of being sold to  
• Have seen every outreach template  

What they rarely receive:

Genuine appreciation with zero expectation.

The pattern:

• Expect nothing  
• Be specific about impact  
• Show sincere appreciation  

Outcomes:

• 80% don’t respond → fine  
• 15% acknowledge → enough  
• 5% become real connections  

That’s how networks are built.

The key condition:
You must first become someone worth paying attention to—through content, messaging, and value.

Key Insight:
Low-pressure, non-transactional outreach outperforms traditional pitching when targeting high-value individuals.
