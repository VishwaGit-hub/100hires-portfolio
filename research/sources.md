## Summary of Insights

Across all sources, effective cold outreach depends on:

1. Clear and relevant messaging (Berman, Efti)  
2. Structured systems and processes (Jason Bay)  
3. Strong preparation and research (Coleman)  
4. Persistent, multi-touch follow-ups (Ingram, Verity)  
5. Trust and positioning (Welsh)  
6. Reducing perceived risk (Braun)
