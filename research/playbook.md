# Cold Outreach Playbook (B2B SaaS)

## 1. Targeting & Research
- Identify buying signals (hiring, growth, funding)
- Research company priorities and stakeholders
- Map decision-makers before outreach

---

## 2. Messaging Principles
- Be clear, direct, and relevant
- Avoid fake personalization
- Focus on the prospect’s problem, not your product
- Ensure offer and context align

---

## 3. Outreach Structure

### Email
- Simple subject line
- Clear problem or observation
- Specific value proposition
- Single clear CTA

### LinkedIn
- Start with relevance (not pitch)
- Use multi-format outreach (text, video, voice)

---

## 4. Follow-Up System
- Minimum 3 touches per prospect
- Change angle each time
- Use multiple channels (email, LinkedIn, phone)

---

## 5. Conversion Strategy
- Don’t negotiate over email
- Move important conversations to calls
- Expand to multiple stakeholders if blocked

---

## 6. Psychology & Risk
- Buyers choose safe options, not best ones
- Reduce perceived risk:
  - Show proof
  - Break into small steps
  - Make switching easy

---

## 7. Content & Inbound Support
- Content should:
  - Build trust
  - Support outbound messaging
  - Warm up prospects before outreach

---

## Core Principle

Outbound works when:
- messaging is relevant  
- targeting is accurate  
- execution is consistent  

Failure happens when teams rely on:
- templates  
- automation without insight  
- single-touch outreach
